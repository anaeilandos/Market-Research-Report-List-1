<p><p>Marek's Disease Vaccine Market consists of two main types: bivalent and monovalent vaccines. Bivalent vaccines protect against two strains of the Marek's Disease virus, offering broader immunity and enhanced protection for poultry. In contrast, monovalent vaccines target a single strain, providing focused but more limited defense. The choice between these vaccines depends on factors such as local disease prevalence, flock age, and specific production goals, influencing overall market dynamics and demand in the poultry industry.</p></p>
