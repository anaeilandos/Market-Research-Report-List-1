<p><p>The Semiconductor Chemical-mechanical Polishing (CMP) Material Market comprises two primary types: CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive materials that provide the mechanical action necessary for polishing semiconductor wafers, ensuring uniform surface finishes. CMP slurries, on the other hand, are liquid mixtures that contain chemical agents and abrasives, aiding in material removal and enhancing surface quality. Together, these materials are crucial for achieving the precision required in wafer fabrication for advanced semiconductor devices.</p></p>
