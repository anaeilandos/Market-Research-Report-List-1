<p><p>The airport biometric readers market comprises three main types: fingerprint readers, facial recognition readers, and hand geometry readers. Fingerprint readers utilize unique finger patterns for identification, ensuring high accuracy and security. Facial recognition readers analyze facial features and provide touchless identification, enhancing passenger flow. Hand geometry readers measure the shape and size of hands, offering a non-intrusive identification method. Together, these technologies improve airport security, streamline passenger processing, and enhance overall travel efficiency.</p></p>
