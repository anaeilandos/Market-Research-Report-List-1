<p><p>ICU Non-invasive Cardiac Output Monitors (NICOM) are advanced medical devices designed to measure cardiac output without the need for invasive procedures. These monitors utilize various technologies, such as bioreactance and impedance cardiography, to provide real-time data on heart function, fluid status, and overall hemodynamic stability. The non-invasive nature of NICOM makes them particularly valuable in critical care settings, where rapid assessment and continuous monitoring are essential.</p><p>The ICU NICOM market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, a rising geriatric population, and heightened awareness of the benefits of non-invasive monitoring techniques.
