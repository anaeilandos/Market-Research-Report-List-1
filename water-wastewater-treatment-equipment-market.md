<p><p>The Water & Wastewater Treatment Equipment market consists of various technologies, including media filtration equipment and membrane filtration equipment. Media filtration equipment utilizes physical barriers, such as sand or activated carbon, to remove contaminants from water through adsorption and straining. In contrast, membrane filtration equipment employs semi-permeable membranes to separate particles, ions, and molecules based on size and charge. Both types are crucial for ensuring clean water supply and effective wastewater management, addressing environmental concerns and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
